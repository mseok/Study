AAE

  • step 1: update AE - update $\phi, \theta$ according to reconstruction error $$L_i (\phi, \theta, x_i) = - E_{q_\theta(z|x_i)}[log(p_\theta (x_i|z))]$$
  • step 2: update Discriminator - update $\lambda$ according to loss for discriminator $$-V_i(\phi, \lambda, x_i, z_i) = -logd_\lambda (z_i) - log(1 - d_\lambda (q_\theta (x_i)))$$
  • stpe 3: update Generator - update $\phi$ according to loss for discriminator $$-V_i(\phi, \lambda, x_i, z_i) = -log(d_\lambda (q_\phi (x_i)))$$

The result of the training is that the encoder learns to convert the data distribution to the prior distribution, while the decoder learns a deep generative model that maps the imposed prior to the data distribution.
